Case Study

Hello Alice
Defending DEI: A Communications Campaign for Hello Alice
Working with the Hello Alice leadership team was a highlight of my communications career. We worked directly with their CEO and founders, Elizabeth Gore and Carolyn Rodz, to help spread awareness of Hello Alice — a free, AI-powered online platform that helps small business owners launch and grow their ventures. It specifically targets members of underrepresented minority groups, providing funding, educational resources, and a network of likeminded entrepreneurs. As a result of the platform's increasing utility and our work amplifying the founders' unique perspectives, Hello Alice grew to over 1.5 million users.

The platform came under threat amidst the escalating opposition to DEI programs across corporate America. Through our campaign, we elevated Elizabeth Gore and Carolyn Rodz as leading voices in defense of this movement. Hello Alice earned coverage across top-tier publications including Bloomberg, Fortune, and Oprah Daily. We also landed Gore a recurring platform as host of her own podcast on the Yahoo Finance network — which she still hosts today.
"We are doubling down. This win gives us the wind in our sails to go even bigger. We aren't changing our mission."
— Elizabeth Gore, Co-Founder & President, live on Bloomberg Technology
